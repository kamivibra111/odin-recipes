Rosemary-Roasted Chicken

A quick Recipe to Rosemary-Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 bone-in skin on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary
  • Ground black pepper
  • cooking spray
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large online, sliced
  • salt, plus more to taste
  • 4 teaspoons white Sugar
  • 5 medium apples
  • 1 pound Yukon Gold potetoes
  • 2 tablespoons Olive oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat.
  3. Layer apples, potatoes and sauteed onion slices.
  4. Drizzle Olive Olive.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven.
  6. Let the Chicken rest before serving.
